Is it okay to be cash-only, or do I really need to accept cards?

Cash-only simplifies fees but can reduce sales.

The Narrative (Left Column)

The Empathy

Card readers add cost and setup, but turning away card customers can feel risky. You want a simple system that still captures sales.

The Education

Card acceptance usually increases conversion, especially for higher-priced items. Cash-only avoids fees but can reduce impulse buys. Many vendors find that the extra sales outweigh the processing costs.

The Solution

Balance simplicity with customer expectations.

  • Accept cards if your average sale is over $20 or you see many shoppers without cash.
  • Stay cash-only for low volume, but post clear signage to avoid confusion.
  • Track the difference in sales volume before and after adding cards.

The right choice is the one that protects profit and keeps sales flowing.
