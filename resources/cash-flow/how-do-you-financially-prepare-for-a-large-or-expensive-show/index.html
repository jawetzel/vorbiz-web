Big shows

How do you financially prepare for a large or expensive show?

Large shows require a financial plan before you commit.

The Narrative (Left Column)

The Empathy

Big shows promise big traffic but also big costs. It is easy to overextend without a plan.

The Education

Preparation includes budgeting for fees, travel, and inventory, plus setting a realistic sales goal. A cash buffer prevents shortfalls before the event.

The Solution

Build a show-specific budget and savings plan.

  • Set a savings target for booth fees and travel.
  • Estimate inventory costs based on expected traffic.
  • Create a sales goal that covers costs and desired profit.

Planning makes large shows more predictable and less risky.
