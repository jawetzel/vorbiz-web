Tax planning

How do you plan for taxes so you’re not hit with a big bill unexpectedly?

Regular set-asides make tax time manageable.

The Narrative (Left Column)

The Empathy

A large tax bill can be shocking if you are not prepared. You want a system that spreads the cost over the year.

The Education

Setting aside a percentage of each sale for taxes creates a predictable buffer. Estimated quarterly payments can also reduce surprises.

The Solution

Make tax planning part of your weekly routine.

  • Set aside a fixed percentage of every event’s sales.
  • Use a separate savings account for tax money.
  • Check quarterly to adjust the percentage if income changes.

Consistent planning avoids last-minute tax stress.
