Off-season

How do you handle cash flow during the off-season (when there are few markets)?

Off-season cash flow requires planning and alternative revenue.

The Narrative (Left Column)

The Empathy

When markets slow down, expenses keep coming. You want a plan that keeps the business stable.

The Education

Off-season planning includes building reserves in peak months, reducing expenses, and finding alternative sales channels like online orders or workshops.

The Solution

Build a plan before the off-season starts.

  • Save a portion of peak-season profits as a buffer.
  • Reduce variable expenses during low months.
  • Add online or wholesale channels to keep income flowing.

Preparation keeps off-season from becoming a financial crisis.
