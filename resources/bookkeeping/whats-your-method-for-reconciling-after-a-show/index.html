Reconciliation

What’s your method for reconciling after a show?

A quick post-show reconciliation prevents small errors from becoming big ones.

The Narrative (Left Column)

The Empathy

After teardown you are tired, and it is tempting to throw the cash box in the car and deal with it later. That delay makes it harder to remember what actually happened and increases mistakes.

The Education

Reconciliation is simply matching what you sold to what you received. When you reconcile right after the event, you can quickly fix discrepancies between cash, card deposits, and your sales log.

The Solution

Use a short checklist before you leave or as soon as you get home.

  • Count cash, subtract the starting float, and record cash sales total.
  • Export or note card sales totals and fees from your POS.
  • Compare totals to your sales log and note any differences immediately.

A 10-minute reconciliation saves hours of cleanup later.
