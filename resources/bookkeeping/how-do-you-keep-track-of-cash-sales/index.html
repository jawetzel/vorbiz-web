Cash control

How do you keep track of cash sales?

Cash sales require intentional tracking because there’s no automatic record.

A common, reliable system

Most vendors use a simple routine:

  • Start each event with a known amount of cash for change.
  • Record cash sales during the event or track totals.
  • Count cash at the end and subtract the starting amount.

Best practices

  • Keep cash sales logged separately from card sales.
  • Deposit cash into your business account regularly.
  • Avoid spending cash before it’s recorded.

A repeatable routine matters more than the exact method.
