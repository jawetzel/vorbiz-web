POS integration

Do you integrate your point-of-sale with bookkeeping?

Many vendors connect systems, but it isn’t required if summaries stay accurate.

Many vendors choose to connect their sales system to their bookkeeping, but it isn’t required.

Why vendors integrate

Integration can help by:

  • Reducing manual data entry
  • Ensuring sales totals are accurate
  • Separating fees, taxes, and deposits cleanly

A common best practice

Instead of recording every individual transaction, many vendors:

  • Keep detailed sales data in their sales system
  • Record daily or per-event summaries in bookkeeping

This keeps financial reports readable and easier to reconcile.

When manual entry is fine

If you sell infrequently or at a small number of events, manually entering totals after each event works well—as long as you’re consistent.

The key isn’t the tool—it’s having a repeatable process.
