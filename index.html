Vendor‑only sales recording

Record sales fast. Keep items tidy. File taxes without headaches.

Vorbiz is a vendor‑focused mobile app for markets and pop‑ups. Create locations, add items with UPCs and variants, and ring sales in seconds—offline or on. Every account automatically backs up your data to the cloud.

What the app does

Locations

Track where sales happen and which items are available. Each location stores its address, tax settings, and whether prices include tax.

Items

Items have a name, price, and optional UPC. Vorbiz can generate QR labels and Quick Buttons for top sellers. Add variants like size or color to keep lists short.

Sale screen

  • Select the active location; Quick Buttons appear for one‑tap adding.
  • Variant items prompt for the right option.
  • Label scanning instantly adds items to the cart.

Reports

  • Day – detailed list of a single day’s sales (free).
  • Date range – summaries for any span (basic).
  • Sales tax – last month for free users; any month with basic.
  • PDF export of reports (basic).

Privacy

Effective date: August 24, 2025

Vorbiz stores the sales data you enter and backs it up to the cloud. We collect basic diagnostic and usage data to keep things running smoothly. To improve app reliability, we also use Sentry to capture error information, which may include device details and application state when a crash occurs. This information is used solely to diagnose and fix issues.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. You may request data deletion or ask questions by emailing support@vorbiz.net.
