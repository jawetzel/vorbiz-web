Vendor‑only sales recording

Record sales fast. Keep items tidy. File taxes without headaches.

Vorbiz is a vendor‑facing app (not a customer POS) built for markets and pop‑ups. Create locations, add items with UPCs and variants, pin quick buttons, and scan labels to ring items in seconds. Reports summarize your day, date ranges, and sales tax by location.

Offline‑first Fast item entry Clean reports

Coming to Google Play.

What the app does

Locations

Define where sales occur and which items are available there. Each location includes a name, address, and tax settings. Enable tax‑inclusive pricing if your listed price already includes tax; disable it to add tax on top.

Items

Create items with a name, price, and a UPC (12‑digit number). Vorbiz can generate a QR label from the UPC so it always scans back to the correct product. Toggle Quick Button for top sellers.

Add variants (e.g., 8oz / 12oz / 16oz) to keep your item list short. When an item has variants, the sale screen prompts you to pick the right option before adding it to the cart.

Sale screen

  • Select the active location; any Quick Buttons appear for one‑tap adding.
  • If an item has variants, you'll choose the option first.
  • Built‑in label scanning adds items instantly.

Managing sales

From the Sales List, you can update a sale's date or location. If line items or totals need changes, delete and recreate the sale with the correct details.

Reporting

  • Day Report – all sales for a single day.
  • Date Range Report – summarized sales for any period.
  • Sales Tax Report – tax collected by location for easy filing.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • / focuses the search bar
  • Enter adds the top search result
  • Shift+Enter completes the sale
