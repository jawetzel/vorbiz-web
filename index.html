How it works

Locations

Set where you sell and how tax should behave. Toggle tax-inclusive pricing if your sticker price already includes tax.

Items & variants

Create items with price and optional UPC. Print a QR/Barcode and scan to add instantly. Use variants (8oz / 12oz / 16oz) to keep your list tidy. Mark top sellers as Quick Buttons.

Sale screen

  • Pick your active location; Quick Buttons appear for one-tap adding.
  • If an item has variants, you’ll pick the right one before it adds.
  • Scan labels to add items instantly. Works great offline.

Manage & report

  • Sales List — adjust date or location if needed.
  • Day Report — everything you sold today.
  • Date Range — summaries for any period.
  • Sales-Tax Report — tax collected by location, ready to file.

Privacy Policy — Vorbiz

Effective Date: September 08, 2025

Vorbiz (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app and services.

1. Information We Collect

a. User Accounts & Devices

  • Email, name, and login details (via Supabase Authentication)
  • Device identifiers (used for secure sync between devices)

b. App Usage & Error Reporting

  • Error logs, crash reports, and performance data (via Sentry)
  • Analytics related to app function and reliability

c. Vendor Business Data (entered by you, the vendor)

  • Location details (business name, address, tax rates, contact information)
  • Products (names, descriptions, pricing, categories, dimensions, certifications, personalization options, etc.)
  • Sales (dates, amounts, discounts, tax information, refunds)
  • Sale line items (quantities, totals, taxes, product references)

d. Customer Data (entered by you, about your customers)

  • Name, business details (resale certificate, EIN, charity EIN if applicable)
  • Address, phone, email, contact person

e. Communications

  • Messages you send us through in-app help requests (via Brevo Email)
  • Subscription and billing information (via RevenueCat, depending on purchase plan)

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve services (syncing, reporting, account management)
  • Troubleshoot issues and monitor performance
  • Communicate support or important updates
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Maintain compliance with tax and business record requirements

We also use aggregated, de-identified data to:

  • Generate statistical insights (e.g., sales trends by region)
  • Conduct research and business analysis
  • Share with third parties (e.g., agencies, associations) only in anonymized form

3. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell or rent personally identifiable information (PII).
  • We do not give third parties direct access to your records.
  • Any external reporting is aggregated and anonymized.

4. How We Share Information

  • Supabase (authentication, database)
  • RevenueCat (subscription and billing)
  • Brevo (email communications)
  • Sentry (error logging and performance monitoring)

We may share aggregate, anonymized insights with third parties. These insights never include personal identifiers.

5. Your Responsibilities as a Vendor

If you input customer data into Vorbiz, you are responsible for having permission or a lawful basis to collect and use that information. We process that data only on your behalf.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data as long as you maintain an account. You may request deletion of your account and related data at any time (subject to legal obligations).

7. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information (if applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact support@vorbiz.net.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and access controls. No system is 100% secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

9. Children’s Privacy

Vorbiz is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated in-app or on our website.

11. Contact Us

Questions? Email support@vorbiz.net.
