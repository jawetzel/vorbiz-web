Vendor‑only sales recording

Record sales fast. Keep items tidy. File taxes without headaches.

Vorbiz is a vendor‑facing app (not a customer POS) built for markets and pop‑ups. Create locations, add items with UPCs and variants, pin quick buttons, and scan labels to ring items in seconds. Reports summarize your day, date ranges, and sales tax by location.

Offline‑first Fast item entry Clean reports

Coming to Google Play.

What the app does

Locations

Define where sales occur and which items are available there. Each location includes a name, address, and tax settings. Enable tax‑inclusive pricing if your listed price already includes tax; disable it to add tax on top.

Items

Create items with a name, price, and a UPC (12‑digit number). Vorbiz can generate a QR label from the UPC so it always scans back to the correct product. Toggle Quick Button for top sellers.

Add variants (e.g., 8oz / 12oz / 16oz) to keep your item list short. When an item has variants, the sale screen prompts you to pick the right option before adding it to the cart.

Sale screen

  • Select the active location; any Quick Buttons appear for one‑tap adding.
  • If an item has variants, you'll choose the option first.
  • Built‑in label scanning adds items instantly.

Managing sales

From the Sales List, you can update a sale's date or location. If line items or totals need changes, delete and recreate the sale with the correct details.

Reporting

  • Day Report – all sales for a single day.
  • Date Range Report – summarized sales for any period.
  • Sales Tax Report – tax collected by location for easy filing.

Summary

Vorbiz is designed for vendors to record sales. We minimize data collection and do not sell user data. Today, the app works offline‑first and stores your sales data locally on your device. We use the device camera solely to scan labels you choose to print and use. If we later add optional services like error logging or account sync, this policy will be updated and those features will be clearly labeled as optional.

What we collect

  • In‑app content you create (e.g., locations, items, sales, reports) — stored locally on your device.
  • Camera input for scanning your item labels (e.g., UPC/QR). No images or video are saved by Vorbiz.

Planned future features (not active yet):

  • Error logs to help diagnose crashes. If enabled, these may include device model, OS version, app version, and anonymized crash details — never your sales data.
  • User accounts for optional sync/backup. If added, we may collect your email and basic profile to provide the service.

How we use data

  • To operate the app and provide core functionality (recording sales, generating reports).
  • To improve stability and fix issues (if error logging is enabled in the future).
  • To provide optional account features (if accounts are added in the future).

App permissions

  • Camera — used to scan your labels for faster item entry. Required only when you choose to scan; otherwise unused.

Sharing & selling

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not share your sales content with third parties unless you explicitly export/share it.
  • If we later use third‑party services (e.g., crash reporting), we will list each service and its role here.

Storage & retention

  • Your sales data is stored locally on your device and remains until you delete the app data, use an export, or we introduce optional account sync.
  • If optional error logs are added later, they will be retained only as long as necessary to diagnose issues.

Security

Local data stays on your device. If we add account sync, data in transit will be encrypted (HTTPS) and we will publish additional details here.

Children's privacy

Vorbiz is intended for business users and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will update the "Last updated" date and, when changes are material, provide in‑app notice.
